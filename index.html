Let me tell you something about me

Currently, I work as the Chief Data Engineer at Semalytix. I love this position because, on the one hand, I am able to work together with brilliant people and on the other hand have the chance to work with cutting-edge technology to design new processes and algorithms for our big data architecture. I love to work with people, and happy to mentor and encourage people in my area to learn and grow beyond themselves.

Technology has always fascinated me, but I also see the challenges technology brings for many areas of our lives. I love reading good books and scientific papers and are always happy to engage in challenging conversations and discussions.

In my free time, I love to do sports but I also work as a volunteer firefighter in my community to help other people as well as I can. I also have a background as a paramedic.

I hold a Ph.D. in computer science from Bielefeld University. In my thesis I worked on the automatic generation of multilingual and linguistically valid lexica, using NLP and AI, which can be used to bridge the gap between written text and factual knowledge in a database. The result can help to improve systems like Siri, Alexa, or Cortana.
